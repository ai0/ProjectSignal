## Features

* Know your steps walked yesterday and today at a glance
* Realtime steps and activity feedback
* Play the Devoured once achieved the steps goal
* Walk more steps to exchange lives for game
* Haptic feedback

## Environment

* Xcode 11.1
* Swift 5.1
